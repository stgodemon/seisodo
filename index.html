About Us

Seisodo Corp. is an international trading company based in Tokyo, Japan.

We support overseas customers and business partners through product sourcing, procurement coordination, export arrangements, and Japan-related trade services.

Our work covers a wide range of Japanese products, including audio accessories, electronic components, hobby items, collectibles, textiles, and other products requested by customers.

Our Services

Japan Product Sourcing

We source products from Japanese online stores, marketplaces, auctions, and specialist retailers according to customer requests.

Procurement Support

We assist with product research, availability checks, quotation support, purchase coordination, and communication with Japanese sellers or suppliers.

Audio Products

We handle audio accessories, cables, connectors, DIY parts, and custom audio-related products.

Japanese Textiles

We support sourcing of kimono fabrics, obi materials, silk textiles, and other traditional Japanese textile products.

Export Coordination

We provide support for international shipping, export preparation, logistics coordination, and related trade procedures.

Custom Requests

We review individual requests based on product type, availability, destination, shipping method, and import conditions.
